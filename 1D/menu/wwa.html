One Direction: Where We Are - The Concert Film

One Direction: Where We Are - The Concert Film is the second film about the band. It's a whole concert and it takes place during the 28 and 29 June 2014 shows of the band's Where We Are Tour at San Siro Stadium in Milan, Italy. It contains a 15-minute interview with the band with exclusive behind-the-scenes footage.
